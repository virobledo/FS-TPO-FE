Attention: if you have already been some time in the gym, you like to train and do not see results, or if you are just entering the world of muscle building and do not know where to start, this book is for you. Learn and master in a clear, easy and effective way everything related to muscle hypertrophy.

  • Learn in a detailed and illustrated manner the 50 main exercises of muscle building
  • Learn to structure a training session based on your objectives
  • Discover which major and secondary muscles participate in each exercise
  • Understand which exercises are potentially harmful and how to modify them
  • Use all the variables of load training in a practical and effective way: intensity, duration, volume, density, frequency, selection of exercises, rest times
  • Conquer and learn to use the 4 factors that induce muscle development: Mechanical Stress, Metabolic Stress, Time Under Stress, Muscular Damage
  • Master the main methods of training: direct series, same muscle two-part training series, two-part training antagonist series, three-part training series, giant sets, drop sets, preexhaust, post-exhaust, ascending pyramids, descending pyramids, triangle pyramids, burn, failure, forced, negative, bulgarian, german, rest pause, cluster, HST, and much more...
  • Learn the best training techniques to develop each muscle group.
  • Understand what factors are limiting your muscle gains and how to avoid them.
  • Learn what to eat to achieve maximum muscle growth with our exclusive sports nutrition guide.
  • Power your muscle gains to the fullest with our sports supplementation guide.

Intelligent Muscle Training is the result of 15 years of sports experience and professional training within the world of exercise sciences. A book where both the physical activity professional and the person who has just started in the world of muscle building will have a clear and didactic guide on how to train in an effective and safe manner, understanding each of the training variables for muscular development.

My greatest wish, from my role as teacher and trainer in exercise sciences, is to provide the reading public with important and extremely essential aspects to take into account when starting any training program for muscle development.
